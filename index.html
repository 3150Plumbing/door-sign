How to make your sign

  1. 1Click the title area to type your title (e.g. RULES FOR DEMO:)
  2. 2Click a bullet point to type an instruction. Use + Add Bullet to add more (up to 3).
  3. 3Click Download PNG to save and print your sign.

🖋 Sign prints at 4″×4″ — best printed on a standard printer and taped to the door.
